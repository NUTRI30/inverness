PEACE OF MIND PIERCING

Inverness offers a medically-hygienic piercing system accessible to all. Experience the exclusive Inverness Safety Back™ and fully-enclosed sterile earring capsules, setting a new standard in cleanliness and safety.

At Inverness, piercing transcends mere adornment. It signifies milestones, trust among friends, and celebrates individuality.

We are dedicated to ensuring your piercing experience is seamless and memorable. Focus on creating memories; let us take care of the details.

For half a century, Inverness Corporation has been a trusted name in safe and gentle ear piercing. Founded in America in 1974, we revolutionized ear piercing by introducing a unique, sterile, and user-friendly system that changed the industry standards.

our innovation, ear piercing was often done at home with an ice cube and needle or in a doctor's office with a loud piercing gun. We transformed this experience with our medically-hygienic process that pierces ears in three simple steps: Cleanse, Mark, Pierce. Our fully encapsulated, sterile piercing earrings and exclusive Inverness Safety Back™ ensure the gentlest and safest ear piercing experience available.

Quality and safety have always been at the forefront of our mission. We proudly manufacture all our products in America, adhering to the stringent legal guidelines of both the United States and Europe. This commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of over 200 million people across 70 countries, where our system is used in jewelry stores, department stores, salons, pharmacies, doctorsʼ offices, and body piercing shops.

At Inverness, we continue to lead the way in ear piercing innovation, delivering unparalleled quality and safety that our customers rely on.
